## About Acme Corp

Acme Corp is a Series B SaaS company building workflow automation tools for mid-market professional services firms. We're 200 people, mostly remote, and our platform helps consulting firms, agencies, and accounting practices manage client engagements from intake through delivery. We closed a $42M Series B in late 2025 and are scaling our go-to-market and product org to match. Our customers include over 600 firms across North America, and we're growing ARR at 80% year-over-year.

We're not a "move fast and break things" shop. We care about building systems that actually work — for our customers and for each other.

---

## The Role

We're looking for a **Product Operations Manager** to sit at the intersection of Product, Engineering, and Go-to-Market. This person will own the operational backbone of our product org — the processes, tools, data, and cross-functional rituals that keep 40+ people shipping effectively. You won't be writing code or managing a P&L. You'll be the person who makes sure nothing falls through the cracks and that the teams building our product have what they need to do their best work.

This role reports to the VP of Product and works closely with Engineering Leads, Customer Success, and Revenue Operations.

---

## What You'll Do

- Own and continuously improve our product development lifecycle — from intake and prioritization through launch and post-launch review
- Manage and configure our tooling stack (Jira, Notion, Salesforce) to support product planning, bug tracking, and cross-team visibility
- Build and maintain dashboards and reports that give leadership clear signal on roadmap progress, feature adoption, and operational bottlenecks
- Coordinate cross-functional launches by working with Product, Engineering, Marketing, and Customer Success to ensure nothing ships without documentation, enablement, and a feedback loop
- Run recurring product rituals: sprint reviews, quarterly planning prep, roadmap syncs, and retrospectives
- Manage vendor relationships for product tooling — evaluate, negotiate, and onboard new tools as needed
- Document and standardize processes across the product org so that tribal knowledge becomes shared knowledge
- Partner with Revenue Operations to ensure product data flows cleanly into Salesforce and our reporting infrastructure

---

## Required Qualifications

- 4+ years of experience in operations, business analysis, or program management within a SaaS or technology company
- Hands-on experience administering at least one of: Jira, Salesforce, or similar business systems — not just using them, but configuring workflows, permissions, and automations
- Demonstrated ability to build reporting and dashboards (SQL knowledge a plus, but we care more about the thinking than the tool)
- Experience coordinating cross-functional projects involving 3+ teams without direct authority over any of them
- Strong written communication — you'll be writing process docs, status updates, and executive summaries regularly
- Comfort working in a remote-first environment with async communication norms

---

## Preferred Qualifications

- Experience at a company between 100–500 employees during a growth phase
- Familiarity with product analytics tools (Amplitude, Mixpanel, or similar)
- Background in consulting, e-commerce, or another field outside pure tech — we value operators who've seen how different businesses run
- Basic scripting ability (Python, Google Apps Script, or similar) for automating repetitive tasks — you don't need to be an engineer, but comfort with light automation goes a long way
