# About Section Structure & Optimization

## Character Limits

| Constraint | Value |
|------------|-------|
| **Maximum** | 2,600 characters |
| **Optimal Range** | 1,800-2,200 characters (300-350 words) |
| **"See more" Truncation** | ~300 characters on desktop |
| **Mobile Truncation** | ~150-200 characters |

## Critical Zone: First 300 Characters

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  CRITICAL: LinkedIn truncates at 300 chars                       │
│                                                                 │
│  Your first 300 characters must contain:                        │
│  1. Hook (creates curiosity or states mission)                  │
│  2. Value proposition (one compelling line)                     │
│  3. Immediate credibility signal                                │
│                                                                 │
│  If the hook doesn't work, they never click "see more"          │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

## Five-Part Structure

### Part 1: Hook (First 1-2 sentences, ~100 chars)

**Purpose:** Stop the scroll, create curiosity

**Hook Types:**

| Type | Example |
|------|---------|
| **Mission Statement** | "I help companies automate what shouldn't be manual." |
| **Surprising Stat** | "In 2024, I automated 70% of my company's manual processes." |
| **Question** | "What if your ops team could do 3x the work without hiring?" |
| **Contrarian Take** | "Most AI projects fail. Mine don't. Here's why." |
| **Story Opener** | "Two years ago, I left [Previous Employer] to build something different." |

**Bad Hooks (Avoid):**
- "I am a passionate professional who..."
- "Results-driven business analyst with..."
- "Experienced consultant seeking..."

### Part 2: Why (2-3 sentences, ~200 chars)

**Purpose:** Connect emotionally, show motivation

**Structure:**
```
[What drives you professionally]
[Why this matters to you personally]
[Connection to your target audience]
```

**Example:**
```
After watching companies waste hundreds of hours on manual processes that
could be automated in days, I made it my mission to fix that. I believe
every operations team deserves tools that actually work.
```

### Part 3: Expertise (3-5 bullets, ~400 chars)

**Purpose:** Establish credibility, show range

**Structure:**
```
What I bring to the table:

• [Domain expertise 1]: [Specific example]
• [Domain expertise 2]: [Specific example]
• [Technical skill]: [How I apply it]
• [Unique combination]: [What makes it valuable]
```

**Example:**
```
My expertise spans:

• Operations Design: Building workflows that scale from 10 to 10,000 users
• AI Implementation: Turning complex AI capabilities into practical tools
• Process Automation: Identifying and eliminating manual work systematically
• Consulting Background: [Consulting Firm]-trained problem solving meets startup speed
```

### Part 4: Proof (3-5 bullets with metrics, ~500 chars)

**Purpose:** Back up claims with evidence

**Structure:**
```
Recent impact:

• [Achievement with metric] at [Company/Context]
• [Achievement with metric] at [Company/Context]
• [Achievement with metric] at [Company/Context]
```

**Example:**
```
What I've delivered:

• Automated 70% of manual operations for a custom clothing company, enabling 3x growth without hiring
• Built AI-powered lookbook generator saving 15+ hours per photoshoot
• Reduced client onboarding time from 2 weeks to 2 days through workflow automation
• Designed measurement tracking system handling 500+ client records with zero errors
```

### Part 5: Call-to-Action (1-2 sentences, ~150 chars)

**Purpose:** Tell reader what to do next

**CTA Options:**

| Situation | CTA |
|-----------|-----|
| **Job Searching** | "Currently exploring AI implementation roles. Let's connect if you're building something interesting." |
| **Building Network** | "I share weekly insights on operations automation. Follow for practical tips." |
| **Consulting/Services** | "Want to automate your ops? DM me or book a call: [link]" |
| **General** | "Let's connect: [email] or send a connection request." |

**Avoid:**
- No CTA at all (waste of space)
- Generic "Let's connect!" (says nothing)
- Multiple CTAs (confusing)

## Voice & Tone Guidelines

### Do Use:
- First person ("I", "my", "me")
- Active voice ("I built", not "was built by me")
- Specific numbers and examples
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Bullet points for lists

### Don't Use:
- Third person ("[Your Name] is a...")
- Passive voice
- Vague claims without evidence
- Wall-of-text paragraphs
- Generic phrases ("passionate", "driven")

## Validation Checklist

Before outputting About section:

- [ ] Within 2,600 characters (target 1,800-2,200)
- [ ] First 300 chars contain hook + value prop
- [ ] Uses first-person voice throughout
- [ ] Contains 3+ quantified achievements
- [ ] All claims verifiable in CONTEXT files
- [ ] Has clear CTA at the end
- [ ] No generic phrases ("passionate", "team player")
- [ ] Bullet points break up text
- [ ] Appropriate for user's situation (job search, networking, etc.)

## Situation-Specific Variations

### Job Searching

- Lead with target role in hook
- Emphasize relevant achievements
- CTA: Open to opportunities

### Building Personal Brand

- Lead with unique perspective/mission
- Emphasize thought leadership
- CTA: Follow for content

### Career Change

- Hook: Bridge old → new
- Emphasize transferable skills
- Proof: Show relevant achievements from old career

### Returning to Workforce

- Hook: What you've been doing (positively framed)
- Emphasize skills maintained/developed
- CTA: Ready to contribute
